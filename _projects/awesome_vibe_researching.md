A curated **awesome list** of 107 resources (45 papers, 62 tools) for **Vibe Researching** — AI-driven research automation across the scientific discovery pipeline.

### Coverage

The list covers six research stages and six autonomy levels:

**Research Stages:**
- Survey, Ideation, Experiment, Analysis, Writing, Promotion

**Autonomy Levels:**
- L0 Manual through L5 Fully Autonomous

### Related Work

The list extends the "vibe coding" concept to the entire research workflow and references the paper *"Vibe Researching as Wolf Coming"* (arXiv:2602.22401, Feb 2026) and the VibeX 2026 Workshop at EASE 2026.
