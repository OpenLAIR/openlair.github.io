**Dr. Claw** is a full-stack AI research workspace that helps researchers execute end-to-end projects — from shaping an initial idea to running experiments and preparing publication-ready outputs.

### Key Features

- **Research Lab Dashboard** covering Survey, Ideation, Experiment, Publication, and Promotion stages
- **One-click Auto Research** execution for streamlined workflows
- **100+ built-in research skills** for diverse research tasks
- **Chat-driven pipeline planner** for interactive research planning
- **Multi-agent backend** supporting Claude Code, Gemini CLI, and Codex as execution engines

### Outputs

The pipeline produces survey reports, research ideas, experiment code, analysis results, paper drafts, and presentation slides.
