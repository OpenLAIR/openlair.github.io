Welcome to the **Lehigh AI Research Lab (LAIR)**, led by [Prof. Lichao Sun](https://lichao-sun.github.io/) at [Lehigh University](https://www.lehigh.edu/). Prof. Sun is also an Adjunct Professor at [Mayo Clinic](https://www.mayoclinic.org/).

We are interested in making machine learning and deep learning algorithms more **reliable, robust, efficient, and interpretable**. Our research spans:

- **Trustworthy AI** — robustness, fairness, privacy, and safety of AI systems
- **Medical AI** — AI-powered healthcare and clinical applications
- **Generative AI / LLM** — large language models, AIGC, and their responsible deployment
- **Federated Learning** — privacy-preserving collaborative machine learning
- **Reinforcement Learning** — efficient and safe decision-making algorithms

Our work directly benefits applications in computer vision, natural language processing, graph networks, and privacy-preserving machine learning systems.

Prof. Sun received his Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Illinois at Chicago in 2020, under the supervision of [Prof. Philip S. Yu](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=D0lL1r0AAAAJ). He was the recipient of the **2024 OpenAI Researcher Access Program Award**, **2024 Microsoft Accelerate Foundation Models Research Award**, **2023 SIGIR Best Paper Honorable Mention Award**, and the **NSF CRII Award**.
